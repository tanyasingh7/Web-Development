Goa

Our first stop was in Goa! It was my first time in Goa and I had always been dying to go! Unfortunately, we went during Monsoon season, so the resort was pretty empty. However, it was still beautiful!

We stayed at The Leela resort and it was beautiful! All of the rooms were in small hut like cabins, and they were gorgeous! Each room had an outside patio with views of a calming lake. In Goa, we visited the famous forts that have been preserved from the seventeenth century. Additionally, we visited some of the famous beaches and churches.
