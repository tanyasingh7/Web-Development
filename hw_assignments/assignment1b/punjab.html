Punjab

We finally made our way to Punjab! Both of my parents are from Punjab and we visit quite often. We first went to Chandigarh, where we met up with some family friends. We went on a beautiful tram ride and went into the mountains and ate a meal. We got to go to a gorgeous rooftop pool and eat lots of good food! After Chandigarh, we traveled to Patiala, where my grandparents live. We spent a majority of our time there, as that is where our family lives. My grandparents threw me a huge graduation party and I had such an amazing time. Pictures

After Patiala, we went to Amritsar to visit the Golden Temple and the Baga Border. It was breathtaking! The Golden Temple is so beautiful and peaceful. The Baga border is the border between India and Pakistan, and daily they have a ceremony when they close the border. It was so cool.
