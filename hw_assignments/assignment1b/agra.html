Agra

We traveled to Agra to see the one and only Taj Mahal. I’ve been to the Taj Mahal before, but I wanted to see it again, and go inside of it again. It was such a hot day and it was packed! There were tourists everywhere.

The Taj Mahal is beautiful. I remember just staring at it and not believing that it was real.
