Mumbai

In Mumbai, we stayed at the Taj Hotel. It is right outside of the India Gate. Our room had a beautiful view of the water and gate.

In Mumbai, we visited a lot of our family friends. We went sightseeing and saw the huge mansions of many Indian celebrities. We went on a tour of the city, and got to hear about the history of Mumbai (Bombay) from a tour guide. We got to swim in the beautiful pool of the Taj Hotel as well. The food there was incredible!
